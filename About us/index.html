History

On a rainy afternoon, Sarah and Alex, the Co-founders of Rainy Days, shared the vision. the vision gave birth to Rainy Days, the place where functional meets fashionable, where waterproof meets wonderful. Rainy days is not just about rainy jackets it is about the people who wear them. whether you are running errands in the city or embarking on a wilderness adventure we believe you should do it in style. that is why their jackets are designed to be versatile, comfortable, and above all reliable. As you are explore our collection of rain jackets, remember that you are not just buying a piece of clothing your are becoming part of the RAINY DAYS story .
